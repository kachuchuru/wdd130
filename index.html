Tanzania

The Olduvai Gorge is located in Tanzania in the Great Rift Valley, near Serengeti National Park and is considered as 'The Cradle of Mankind' because many very old human fossils and tools were discovered there. These discoveries helped scientists learn how early humans made tools, lived and hunted and how evolution developed over millions of years.
