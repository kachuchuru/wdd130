History

Our Splash Whitewater rafting company started about 20 years ago as a small family venture along the Rufiji River, guided by a passion for adventure and nature. Over the years, it has grown into a trusted destination for thrill-seekers and outdoor lovers.

Today, we offer a variety of rafting experiences, from beginner friendly trips to challenging rapids. Our mission is to provide safe, exciting, and unforgetable adventures for all our guests. Our future plans is to start similar activities on Kagera River.
